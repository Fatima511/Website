Attab Village

Attab Village is one of the historic villages in the Northern State of Sudan. The village faces some developmental challenges that our association works to address through sustainable projects that meet the needs of residents and align with their aspirations for a better future.

Discover more about the village

Our Projects

We are working on several projects to serve the Attab village community

Children's Kindergarten

Building a modern kindergarten to provide early education for children in the village, helping them develop their skills.

Medical Center

Establishing a health center to provide basic medical services to villagers, including maternal and child health services and emergency care.

Youth & Sports Center

Creating a center for youth activities and sports to develop skills and talents, and provide healthy recreational opportunities for village youth.
